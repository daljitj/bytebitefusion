Moroccan Style Pizza

Send your tastebuds on an exotic adventure without leaving the comfort of your home kitchen with this Moroccan Style Pizza. This recipe builds upon a traditional pizza by introducing a rich medley of Moroccan flavors, carrying an unparalleled culinary experience right in every bite. The base begins with a soft, expertly kneaded dough, stretched to thin perfection. Instead of the conventional tomato base, this pizza tantalizes your palate with a unique and mildly spiced Moroccan chermoula sauce; a flavorful blend of herbs, garlic, preserved lemons, and vibrant spices that seep into the fluffy dough beneath.
